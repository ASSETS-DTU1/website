INVESTMENT WING

In this wing, we discuss and share resources to learn about Stock Market and other Investments. Incase, you are a beginner we will provide you with all the resources required for you to get started with investing!

There is really active community which discuss and analysis all the stock maket trends on a daily basis. Experienced seniors will also conduct regular informative meets and session to help you learn about investing!

Technical Wing :

This wing mainly has 2 sections namely Quant and Data Science. In Quant Section, we focus on algorithmic trading and quantitative finance. In Data Science we share resources which will help you to learn Data Science and Data Analysis.

Besides this, the members also work on various industry level projects related to Data Science and ML which are lead by a team of experts which will guide you throughout your journey

Crypto Department

We encourage discussions about Cryptocurrencies and Blockchain in this Department. There is a really active community which discusses about Blockchain, Altcoins, NFTs and all the other applications of Blockchain in the world of finance.

Competition Department :

Members will get a chance to represent ASSETS DTU in various competitions held on D2C and DU Circuit We will organise mock competitions, provide resources and seniors might take sessions related to competitions!
