blog-1 COULD THE 2008 RECESSION BE PREVENTED?

The Great Recession, often known as the financial crisis of 2008, was the biggest economic crisis after the 1930s great depression and resulted in a 4% decline in the US GDP along with a 3% decline in the world GDP growth rate in just two years. 37 million unemployment claims, 170,000 more homeless families, and these are just figures for the United States. On a global scale, this crisis wreaked havoc. What was the reason behind this crisis? Could this crisis have been predicted? Could we have done something to prevent it? Join us as we dive deep into the 2008 financial crisis and try to answer all these questions. Check out ASSETS DTU's latest medium article on the topic : COULD THE 2008 RECESSION BE PREVENTED?
