ABOUT US

ASSETS DTU is the Finance and Investment Society of Delhi Technological University (Formerly Delhi College of Engineering).

ASSETS DTU aims to nurture financially prudent students and start an investing culture. ASSETS is a society that focuses on imparting a basic understanding of â€˜how to manage your money. Our vision is to create the next generation of smart and financially prudent individuals and we are on the path to spread financial literacy.

There are many departments in ASSETS DTU like :

Investment Department - where we discuss and share resources to learn about Stock Market and other investments.

Crypto Department - We encourage discussions about Cryptocurrencies and Blockchain in this Department.

Technical Department - These Department mainly has 2 sections namely Quant and Data Science. In Quant Section, we focus on algorithmic trading and quantitative finance. In Data Science we share resources which will help you to learn Data Science and Data Analysis.
